Backtest Methodology — Fully Transparent

All calculations are performed in-browser JavaScript from raw weekly price data. No pre-computed results.

1. Data: Weekly Friday closing prices for 36 S&P 500 stocks + S&P 500 index from FMP API (Jan 2021 → Mar 2026, 266 weeks).

2. Signal: For each stock at week w, compute 4-week momentum = (price[w] / price[w-4] - 1) × 100.

3. Selection: Rank all stocks by 4-week momentum descending. Select top N.

4. Weighting: Momentum-weighted. Shift scores so minimum = 1: shifted[i] = momentum[i] - min(all) + 1. Normalize: weight[i] = shifted[i] / sum(shifted).

5. Return: Portfolio return for week = Σ weight[i] × (exit_price[i] / entry_price[i] - 1).

6. Rebalance: Every Friday (every row in the data). Entry = Friday close week w. Exit = Friday close week w+1.

7. Beta: Cov(strategy_weekly_returns, spy_weekly_returns) / Var(spy_weekly_returns) over last 52 weeks.

8. Alpha (Jensen's): (mean_strategy - beta × mean_spy) × 52 = annualized excess return, in percent.

9. S&P Benchmark: Real ^GSPC weekly Friday close values. Growth computed identically: $100K × Π(1 + weekly_spy_return).

10. No look-ahead bias: At week w, only prices up to and including week w are used. The trade is entered at week w close and exited at week w+1 close.
